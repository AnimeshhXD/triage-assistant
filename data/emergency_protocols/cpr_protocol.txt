CARDIOPULMONARY RESUSCITATION (CPR) PROTOCOL

Indications:
- Unresponsive patient with no normal breathing and no pulse.

Immediate actions:
- Call for help and activate emergency response system.
- Begin high-quality chest compressions at a rate of 100-120 per minute.
- Provide rescue breaths with appropriate airway adjuncts when available.

Red flags:
- Prolonged downtime.
- Known terminal illness or valid do-not-resuscitate order.

Ongoing management:
- Continue cycles of compressions and ventilations.
- Apply defibrillator or AED as soon as possible.
