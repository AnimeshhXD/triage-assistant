BURN EMERGENCY PROTOCOL

Indications:
- Thermal, chemical, or electrical burns.

Immediate actions:
- Remove patient from source of injury.
- Cool the burn with cool (not ice) water.
- Assess total body surface area (TBSA) burned and burn depth.
- Provide analgesia.

Red flags:
- Burns involving face, hands, genitalia, or major joints.
- Inhalation injury or airway compromise.

Ongoing management:
- Consider fluid resuscitation for major burns.
- Arrange transfer to burn center when indicated.
