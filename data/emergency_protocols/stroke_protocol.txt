STROKE EMERGENCY PROTOCOL

Indications:
- Sudden facial drooping.
- Sudden arm weakness or leg weakness.
- Sudden speech difficulty or confusion.

Immediate actions:
- Activate stroke team and record time last known well.
- Check blood glucose immediately.
- Obtain non-contrast CT head as soon as possible.
- Maintain airway, breathing, and circulation.

Red flags:
- Severe hypertension.
- Rapidly worsening neurologic status.

Ongoing management:
- Evaluate for thrombolysis or thrombectomy per local guidelines.
